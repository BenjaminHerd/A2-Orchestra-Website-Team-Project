Goals

Improve exposure and as a result, enitice more people to join the barrier reef orchestra. Therefore, the design has to draw the viewers attention to the orchestra's and generate exposure to the wider public in general. On the main page, the client wants a focus and ease of access for people to join easily, possible fundraising element in play as well. Main problem was too much text on front page, there is a lot of information the client wants, so be wary.

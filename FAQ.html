FAQs

 

How do I sign up to the join the Orchestra?

You can easily sign up the barrier reef orchestra by clicking the Sign up button of the Home page.

 

How can I check for upcoming events or concerts?

Our upcoming events or concerts can be seen on our Events page.

 

Where can I buy tickets for upcoming events or concerts?

You can purchase tickets from our Events page or by going to Ticketek as our concerts become available (notified on our Events page).

 

Where can I find photos of the recent events the Barrier Reef Orchestra was in?

You can find the photos taken by our crew and willing photographers alike on our Gallery page.
